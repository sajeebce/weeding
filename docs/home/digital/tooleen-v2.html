FAQ

Things people ask
before signing up.

How do I know the accounts are legitimate?
+
Every account is a genuine licensed seat purchased directly from the provider — Anthropic, OpenAI, Figma, etc. You'll receive an invitation email from the actual company, not from us. Log in with your own credentials to the native app. We're not reselling API access or using proxies of any kind.
What exactly happens after I subscribe?
+
After payment, you'll land on your Tooleen dashboard. Add your team members' email addresses and they'll each receive invitations from each tool provider within 2 hours. Everyone sets up their own password and has their own private account.
Can I mix and match which tools my team gets?
+
Not yet — the bundle is all-or-nothing by design, which is how we keep the price down. A "choose your tools" option is on our roadmap for mid-2026. For now, you can just choose not to use the tools you don't need — you're not locked into anything.
What if someone leaves our team?
+
Remove them from your dashboard and their access is revoked within an hour. Their seat can then be reassigned to someone else immediately, without waiting for a billing cycle to change.
Do you offer annual billing?
+
Yes — pay annually and get 2 months free (effectively 17% off). Annual invoices can also be paid by bank transfer if your finance team prefers that. Contact us after signing up and we'll handle the switch.
Is there a refund policy?
+
You have 7 days free to try Tooleen before your card is ever charged. If something goes wrong in the first 30 days after that, contact us and we'll sort it out — we'd rather fix the problem than process a refund.
