FAQ

Honest answers to
the real questions.

Is this legitimate? Are these real tool accounts?
+
Yes. Every seat is a genuine licensed account — the same thing you'd get buying directly from Anthropic, OpenAI, or Notion. We purchase team plans in bulk and pass the savings to you. No shared passwords, no API proxies, no shady workarounds.
How does the 4-person Team plan work exactly?
+
After subscribing, you add your team members' emails to your Tooleen dashboard. Within 2 hours, each person receives their own invitation to Claude, ChatGPT, Notion, and every other tool in the plan. They each use their own account, with their own data and history.
What happens if I need more than 4 seats?
+
Upgrade to our Studio plan (10 seats) or contact us — we can accommodate teams up to 50 people. For teams over 50, we'll build a custom quote. Most requests get a response within a few hours.
Can I try before committing?
+
Yes — all plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. If you decide Tooleen isn't right for you within 7 days, just let us know and you won't be charged.
Do the tools update automatically?
+
Yes. When Anthropic releases a new Claude model or OpenAI updates ChatGPT, your access updates automatically — because these are real accounts, not a layer on top. You're always on the latest version.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
Stripe for card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and we also accept bank transfers for annual plans. We invoice in USD. For annual plans, you get 2 months free.
