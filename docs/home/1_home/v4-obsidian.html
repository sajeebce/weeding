Can I form a US LLC without ever visiting America?
Absolutely. The entire process is 100% remote. You don't need a US visa, green card, or Social Security Number. Over 12,000 international entrepreneurs have formed their LLC through LLCPad without ever stepping foot in America.
Which state should I choose — Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico?
It depends on your goals. Wyoming offers the best combination of low fees, no state income tax, and strong privacy. Delaware is preferred for venture-funded startups. New Mexico has the lowest fees. We help you choose the right state during your free consultation.
How quickly can I start selling on Amazon?
With our Enterprise package, you can have your LLC, EIN, bank account, and Amazon seller application submitted within 5-7 business days. Amazon typically approves new seller accounts in 2-3 weeks after submission.
Will I need to file US taxes?
Non-resident single-member LLC owners with no US-source income typically have minimal tax obligations. You'll file Form 5472 and a pro-forma 1120 annually. We recommend consulting a tax professional for advice specific to your situation.
Can I open Stripe, PayPal, or Wise with my LLC?
Yes! Your LLC with an EIN qualifies you for Stripe, PayPal Business, Wise Business, Mercury, and most major payment processors. We can assist with the application process for each of these services.
What happens if I miss an annual compliance deadline?
That's exactly what our compliance monitoring prevents. We track every deadline and send reminders 60, 30, and 7 days before due dates. If you're on our registered agent plan, we handle the filings for you automatically.
