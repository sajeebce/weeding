Absolutely. US law allows non-citizens and non-residents to form and own LLCs in any state. You do not need a visa, Social Security Number, or US address to get started. Over 12,000 international entrepreneurs from 50+ countries have successfully formed their US LLC through LLCPad — 100% remotely. We recommend Wyoming for approximately 80% of our clients. It offers no state income tax, strong privacy protections, low annual fees ($60/yr), and a business-friendly legal environment. Delaware is preferred if you plan to seek venture capital funding, as many investors are familiar with Delaware law. New Mexico offers the lowest filing fees but fewer long-term advantages. Our specialists will recommend the best state based on your specific business model and goals.

With our Enterprise package, you can go from signup to Amazon Seller application in as little as 5-7 business days. This includes LLC formation (24-48 hrs with rush processing), EIN assignment (1-3 days), and Amazon Professional Seller account application. Brand Registry enrollment typically takes an additional 2-4 weeks after your trademark is filed. We guide you through every step of the process.

Non-resident LLC owners are generally required to file Form 5472 and a pro-forma Form 1120 annually with the IRS, even if no US tax is owed. Single-member foreign-owned LLCs typically do not pay US income tax on income earned outside the US, but tax obligations depend on your specific situation, home country tax treaties, and business activities. We strongly recommend consulting a qualified international tax professional, and our Premium plans include a free consultation.

Yes. Once you have your LLC formation documents and EIN (Employer Identification Number), you can open accounts with Stripe, PayPal Business, Wise Business, and most major payment processors. A registered agent is a person or company designated to receive official legal and government correspondence on behalf of your LLC. Every US state requires LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a physical address in the state of formation. LLCPad provides registered agent service — we receive all documents at our office, scan them, and deliver them to your dashboard within 24 hours. One year of registered agent service is included free with our Professional and Enterprise packages.

Every client gets lifetime access to their LLCPad dashboard where all documents are stored securely.
